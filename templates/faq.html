Go to the /login page and click "Forgot password page." Follow the instructions to reset it.

Only users with the Admin, Director, or Head of Department (HOD) role can upload and approve SOPs.

Go to /amendments, select the SOP, provide details, and submit. Admins/HODs will review it and effect the required changes.

Submit a support ticket on the /help page or email {{ settings.admin_email }}.

Navigate to the SOP list, find the document you need, and click the 📥 Download link next to it.

Select the checkboxes next to the SOPs and click “✅ Mark selected as Read”
✅ Multiple SOPs can be selected and mark as read.

Admins, Director, and Heads of Department automatically receive notifications when new amendments are submitted or closed.

After logging in, go to the Change Password page from your account menu, enter your new password, and save the changes.
